Supporting Families

Gendered Intelligence is committed to identifying the issues and meeting the needs of parents and family members of young trans people from across the UK. We have in-person meetings as well as our new online discussion group for parents, family members, partners, friends, and allies of young trans people. For more information check out this section, or email our Parent Volunteers on parents@genderedintelligence.co.uk.

  • Family & Friends Meetings - For parents, family members, friends and partners of young trans people to meet, chat and gain mutual support.

  • Parent Volunteers who can offer support and a chat to other parents and family members of young trans people over email.

  • Online discussion group - For parents and family members of young trans people to chat online and gain support between meetings.

  • Resources for parents on supporting young trans people.
