Regular donations

Becoming a regular giver by Standing Order is one of the best ways you can help Gendered Intelligence continue its work improving the lives of trans people. It helps us to plan ahead, and it cuts our administration costs, meaning that more money can go directly into our work. You can set up a standing order by using Localgiving - Donating through Localgiving means that we receive an additional 25p for every £1 that you give!

Donate by text

If you wish to donate by text, please text 'TRANSLIVES 5' to 70085, to donate £5 a month. You can replace the '5' with any number you want to donate e.g 'TRANSLIVES 3' or 'TRANSLIVES 10'.

Text cost £5 (or whichever amount you choose to donate) plus two standard rate messages, and you'll be opting in to hear more about our work and fundraising via telephone and SMS. If you'd like to donate but do not wish to receive marketing communications, please text 'TRANSLIVESNOINFO 5' to 70085.

Gendered Intelligence Friends Scheme

Become a friend of Gendered Intelligence by donating monthly to our Friends Scheme. We want to make a difference by increasing opportunities, building skills, and empowering trans youth. Donating monthly to our organisation means that you aid our trans youth support work and volunteering scheme continuously - and it doesn't have to cost you more than a cup of coffee and a morning pastry! Pick one of the categories below or contact us about a set amount you'd like to give. In return, we will send you updates on what your donations are helping achieve at Gendered Intelligence and what we're up to (if you choose).

  • Gold Friend - £3 a month subscription. Your donation helps us provide refreshments for our trans youth group meetings. Even a donation as little as £3 can aid our organisation in providing a quality space for trans youth.

  • Platinum Friend - £10 a month subscription. Your donation provides travel bursaries for trans youth who may need assistance attending our monthly meetings.

  • Diamond Friend - £25 or more a month subscription. Your donation goes towards keeping things running behind the scenes. Staff costs, facilitators, building costs, etc. all allow us to provide quality spaces for trans youth.

If you would like to donate to our Friends Scheme, please click the button below. If you would like to talk to us about your donation, please use our website contact form.
