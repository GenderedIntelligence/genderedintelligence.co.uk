Gendered Intelligence is a registered charity in England and Wales (No. 1182558)


Our work is funded in several ways. We receive:

  • Donations from members of the public
  • Payments for services we deliver, such as training and consultancy
  • Funding from charitable trusts and corporate donors


We are grateful to our many generous supporters. Thank you!
