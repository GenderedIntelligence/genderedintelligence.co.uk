11-15 Youth Group in London

Please note, all GI groups and youth service events will now take place in a mixture of in person and online settings.

Gendered Intelligence runs a youth group for trans, non-binary, gender variant and gender questioning young people aged under 16. The starting age for the group is 11 years old.

If you are under 11, please see the our Under 12s group or email our Youth Work lead finn.greig@genderedintelligence.co.uk.

In Person sessions runs on the third Wednesday of each month.

Online sessions run on the second Friday of each month.

Meetings take place at Caxton House Community Centre in Archway, 129 St John's Way, N19 3RQ.
