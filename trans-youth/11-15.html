Under 16 Youth Group in London

Gendered Intelligence runs a youth group for trans, non-binary, gender variant and gender questioning young people aged under 16. The starting age for the group is 8 years old.

The session runs once a month on a Thursday.

There is no group in August 2015.

Meetings are 5.30-7.30pm (meet at Caledonian Road tube station at 4.45-5pm or if you know where you're going we'll see you there). Parents are welcome to stay until 5.30pm to talk to our worker and get to know us.

Meetings take place at the Underground centre in Holloway, Islington, N7 8TQ.
