Under 12s group in London

Please note all GI youth groups and youth service events will now take place in a mixture of online and in person settings.

Our under 12s group is for trans and questioning young people aged 8-11. 7 year olds can come to online and in person groups with prior arrangement. Siblings aged 7-11 are also welcome.

The group will take place on 2nd Saturday of the month. Please check "Upcoming Dates" below.

Time: 12-3pm

Please email youthwork@genderedintelligence.co.uk if you want to sign up or have further questions.

Group for parents and carers

Parents and carers of the young people in the under 12s group are invited to take part in a group we run alongside the youth group. It's a chance to meet other parents and carers, discuss issues that affect you and get peer support.

The group takes place in the same venue as the under 12s group.

If the usual under 12s group is being replaced by a trip or special event, the parent/carers group might not take place. Please check the specific information for each month.

Who can come to our under 12s group?

No matter how you identify, Gendered Intelligence's our under 12s youth groups is open to all young trans, non-binary, gender diverse and quesitoning people between 8-11 year old (if you have a trans child who is under 8, please contact us for more information and support).

We strive to be fully inclusive of individuals who identify as non-binary, as well as male or female.

You do not have to be accompanied by a parent or carer, however if you are under 18, a youth worker will need to take the contact details of a next of kin in case of emergencies. There is no need to register in advance - just turn up on the day.

Please email youthwork@genderedintelligence.co.uk if you want to sign up or have further questions.
