13-21 year olds youth group in Leeds

Please note, in light of the current situation and until further notice, all GI youth groups and youth service events will be held online.

The first Gendered Intelligence group outside of London, we set up our Leeds youth group in October 2014. We welcome all trans, non-binary, gender variant and questioning young people aged 13 or over and under 21.

We also hold a group in Bradford quarterly, in connection Equity Partnership. Please email youthwork@genderedintelligence.co.uk for more details.
