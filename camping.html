GI Camps 2022 – Information

Bookings will open on Monday 25th of April

Our summer camp booking will be going live on Monday 25th April at 6pm! It will be open for three weeks.

This year our camps will be:

11-17 year olds 12 - 15th August (Saturday 2nd July prep day, you must attend prep day to attend camp)

16-26 year olds 22nd - 25th July (Saturday 25th June prep day, you must attend prep day to attend camp)

Camps are at no cost to young people. However, donations are very welcome and will contribute to costs of future residentials.

This year booking will primarily be first come first served. We have 35 spaces available for both camps. 10 spaces will be reserved for people who have not been camping with us before. We will also reserve a minimum of 7 spaces for trans people of colour, and a minimum of 7 spaces for transfeminine young people at each camp.

We know that booking a space can feel stressful to many and young people often ask for what information they need to have at hand when booking. Here’s what you need:

  • Basic information (name, email, date of birth, age on camp, address, which camp you’d like to attend)
  • Health, medical and access needs (what medication you will bring with you to camp, underlying medical conditions, allergies, GP name and postcode, dietary needs, access needs)
  • Emergency contact & legal guardian (name and address of parent/legal guardian, two emergency contacts and their phone number)
  • What are you hoping to gain from camp or what you are hoping it will be like


We know that the past few years have been really hard and we would take everyone on camp if we could. We are currently working hard to build even more residentials and other opportunities for young trans, non-binary and gender-questioning people across the UK.

If you have any worries, questions or concerns, please talk to a youth worker, or email us on: youthwork@genderedintelligence.co.uk
