Information needed for your booking

You will need to complete a form to book your place. The information you will need is below.

Please email us on youthwork@genderedintelligence.co.uk if you need support with this.

This year, booking is NOT first come, first served. Instead, you will need to complete the section on the form regarding your motivation to join. You can do so by replying to the following question on the booking form: Why do you want/ need to come on camp this year?

To complete the booking form, you will need the following information
  • An email address and phone number we can use to contact you
  • Any health needs or access needs
  • Any allergies
  • Any medications (including name, amounts and when you take them)
  • The name and postcode of your GP surgery
  • The names and phone numbers of 2 people we can contact about you in an emergency (they must be over 18 years old and live in the Uk)
