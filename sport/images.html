Here you can download our free sports images and icons themed around the trans, non-binary, and agender colours. We acknowledge the generous support of Sport England in enabling us to commission and provide these images free of charge.


  • Trans Sports Icons
  • Non-binary Sports Icons
  • Agender Sports Icons

    • Any organisation seeking to be trans inclusive is welcome to download as many of these images as they wish. However, if other organisations want copies, please signpost them to this page rather than passing the images on directly. Tracking the number of downloads helps us get an idea of how much they are used and influences whether we invest in more!

    If there's a particular image that you want but we don't have, let us know via our contact form and we may be able to add it in future.
