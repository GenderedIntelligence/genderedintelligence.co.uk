Imagining Our Futures 2020

This year we are hosting two separate events for trans, gender diverse and questioning young people. The first will be on Work and the second on Families. Imagining Our Futures - Work, is coming up on Saturday, 25th of April! Imagining Our Futures - Families, will take place on Saturday 16th May.

Young people who want to take part

Please email youthwork@genderedintelligence.co.uk to get an invitation link for the events

Applying to be a panellist at Imagining our Futures 2020

Hearing from trans adults on our panel at Imagining Our Futures can completely turn around the way young trans people think about their future at work and their ideas about future families.

Work

We are looking for trans/non-binary/gender diverse adults from diverse backgrounds to volunteer to speak on a panel about their experiences of working or pursuing their interests. We are aiming for a range of work backgrounds as we know that trans people work across all sectors. We also welcome panellists to talk about experiences that are not based on paid employment, for example volunteering or activism. Please find the form to apply to be a panellist for our Work event here

Families

We are looking for trans/non-binary/gender diverse adults from diverse backgrounds to volunteer to speak on a panel about their experiences around everything to do with their family, whether that be them and their dog, a trans man who's had a baby, or a group of trans people who consider themselves one big chosen family. We know that 'the traditional family' can serve as a beautiful template for trans people too, and are keen to see that represented well. Please find the form to apply to be a panellist for our Families event here

Information about the events

Layout: From 2pm, there will be a panel of trans and/or non-binary adults talking about their experience of being trans at work or while pursuing other projects such as campaigning

After the discussion there will be a chance to talk to our guests in smaller groups and answer any questions you have.

Date: Saturday, 25th April 2020 (Work) and Saturday 16th May 2020 (Families)

Time: 1.30pm - 5pm

Venue: Online - link to meeting will be sent closer to the time

Age range: 13-24
