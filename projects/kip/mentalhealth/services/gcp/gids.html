Gender Identity Development Service

The Gender Identity Development Service (GIDS) is currently the national NHS gender care service for under 18s. They operate for four regions: London, Belfast, Exeter and Leeds. They work with children and young people, and their families and carers.

The Gender Identity Development Service have an introductory leaflet explaining services available for gender variant young people including information about referring agencies.
