Transitioning

Transitioning is an important word for trans people. It can mean taking the journey from your assigned sex/gender to the one you feel yourself to be or feel more comfortable expressing. Often trans people talk about socially transitioning. This means taking the steps such as changing your name, pronoun, clothes, haircut and other aspects of your appearance.
