Resources

Gendered Intelligence has produced a number of reports and resources which will be useful for young trans people, their families, and those who work with them. Check the links below for our full range of resources available.

  • Gendered Intelligence Reports & Resources - Reports outlining the work we do with young trans people.
  • Resources for Trans Youth - Resources for young trans people to gain information and support.
  • Resources for Families - Our family support booklet and report in to issues faced by families of young trans people.
  • Resources for Professionals - Information for professionals who work with trans people.
  • Trans Community Conference Reports - Reports from our trans community conferences.
  • Resources produced by other organisations - Many other organisations have produced useful resources for trans people and those who work with them. You can find links to them here.
