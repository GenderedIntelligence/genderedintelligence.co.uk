--- title: Transfemme Group ---

Transfemme Group for trans women, trans girls and transfeminine people aged 13-25

Please note, all GI groups and youth service events will now take place in a mixture of in person and online settings.

Transfemme group, aka “Young gender nonconforming/transgender women or non binary humans living between the north & south poles of the planet Earth (YNGTWNHLBN&SPOFPE)” is Gendered Intelligence’s youth group for trans women, trans girls and non-binary people who are transfeminine aged 13-25.

The group is dedicated to self-identifying trans women, trans girls and transfeminine young people only. All workers, volunteers and visitors identify as transfeminine or as trans women. If you’re unsure if this is the right group for you, email youthwork@genderedintelligence.co.uk

We acknowledge that because of transmisogyny young trans women and transfeminine people sometimes experience additional difficulties. This group is designed to be a safe space for anyone who would benefit from being around other trans women, girls and transfeminine non-binary people. We believe that there’s no such thing as not being ‘trans enough’ or ‘feminine enough’. You are welcome to join the group as you are, no matter what pronouns you use, what clothes you wear, what name you use or how long you have identified as transfeminine.
