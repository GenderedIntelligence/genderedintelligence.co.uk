--- title: Media Enquiries ---

We are happy to receive media enquiries and will do our best to work with journalists across media formats.

As an organisation, we have a broad range of interests and knowledge relating to trans people in the UK and gender diversity more widely. Please find below a non-exhaustive list of subjects that we are happy to speak about:

  • The day to day lives of trans people and the issues that affect them
  • Trans young people and children
  • Non-binary issues
  • Legislation and trans rights
  • Discrimination and hate crime
  • Sports and physical activity
  • Health, including mental health
  • Education, from primary school to higher education
  • Inclusion of trans people in public and cultural life
  • Media and representation
  • Trans and gender diverse actors in theatre, film and TV

If you are looking to speak to a young trans person, please be aware that we consider the wellbeing of the children and young people we work with as a priority. It is highly unlikely that we would welcome filming in any of our youth spaces.

We are working with a group of parents and carers of trans and non-binary children and young people who are interested in speaking about the issues that affect them and their families.

Please send all media enquiries to: publicengagement@genderedintelligence.co.uk
