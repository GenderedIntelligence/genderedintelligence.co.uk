Are you looking for a trans or non-binary actor?

Gendered Intelligence can share your casting call.

From 2015-19, Gendered Intelligence ran TransActing, a project that delivered a range of acting training and industry workshops to trans, non-binary and gender diverse actors aged 16+.

You can find out more about TransActing alumni here.

As a result of this project, we run a Facebook group for all trans actors in our network and we are happy to share casting calls with the group.

If you would like us to share a casting call, please consider that we will be sharing it as a Facebook post. Therefore it would be extremely useful if you could send a short text to share that highlights:

  • The role being advertised and requirements for it
  • If the role is paid, and what the fee is
  • When and where auditions are taking place
  • When the project itself is taking place

Please email all casting calls and any questions to admin@genderedintelligence.co.uk
